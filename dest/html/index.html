    About

    Welcome! I'm a San Diego, CA based frontend developer who loves designing interactive UIs and UXs that delight users. I have a passion for learning and a drive to completely understand how the entire web and all its technologies work. I know this is a long shot but I am going to try anyway ;) I am detail oriented and best-practice focused. I strive to write modular, readable code that is easy to debug and easy for my fellow developers to understand.

    When I'm not at my laptop I can be found surfing, cooking, or in the kitchen whipping up some type of food experiment. Check out a selection of my projects below.
